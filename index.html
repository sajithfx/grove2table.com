How Grove2Table Works

Connecting Orange County neighbors with local farms, home gardens, and select farmers market vendors through our managed delivery network.

1

Browse & Order Online

Browse fresh produce from local farms, home gardens, and select farmers market vendors. Place orders at least 48 hours ahead for guaranteed availability and optimal freshness.

2

We Handle Everything

Our staff coordinate with farmers and gardeners, collect your orders, and manage all the logistics. No extra work for growers - they just fulfill our consolidated orders.

3

Fresh Delivery

Receive your ultra-fresh produce delivered to convenient neighborhood pickup points or direct to your door. Pay fair prices directly supporting local growers.
