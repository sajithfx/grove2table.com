How Grove2Table Works

Working WITH farmers markets to make fresh, local food more accessible while supporting vendors and market communities.

1

Browse & Order

Browse available produce by market day. Place orders at least 48 hours before market day to guarantee availability of your favorite items.

2

Visit the Market

Go to your chosen farmers market on market day. Find your vendor's booth and enjoy the full market experience - discover new vendors, seasonal specialties, and community atmosphere.

3

Pick Up & Pay

Collect your pre-ordered items directly from the vendor's booth. Pay market prices directly to the farmer. Support both the vendor and the farmers market.
